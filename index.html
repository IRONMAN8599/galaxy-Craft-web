About US

We're CyberPixel and we offer

CyberPixel is a Bedrock Only Server. We focus on bringing the best experience to Low, Medium & High End Devices. Our only goal is to make sure our players have the best experience possible. We've been developing gamemodes and bringing Minecraft Java like Server experience to Bedrock. Our servers are optimized for players from any kind of device and that play from anywhere in the world.
